Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or create an account, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address. Payment information is processed securely by Stripe and is never stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To send order confirmations and delivery updates
  • To respond to your inquiries via WhatsApp or email
  • To send marketing emails (only if you opt in)

3. Data Storage

Customer data is stored securely and is never sold, shared, or rented to third parties. We use Resend for transactional emails and Stripe for payment processing — both are GDPR-compliant services.

4. Your Rights

You may request a copy of your data, ask us to correct inaccuracies, or request deletion of your account at any time by contacting us at hello@hkminijungle.com.

5. Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies for shopping cart functionality and language preference. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, email hello@hkminijungle.com or message us on WhatsApp.
