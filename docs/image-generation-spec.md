## Brand Guidelines for Image Generation
- Color palette: Forest green (#1A2A1E base, #4ADE80 accent)
- No fake-looking plastic plants
- No traditional terracotta pots
- No garden center aesthetic
- Yes: metal frames, clean lines, architectural spaces, natural materials
- Lighting: soft, natural, never harsh flash
- Vibe: Kinfolk × Apple × Aesop
