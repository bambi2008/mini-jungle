The Verdict

Plant beginner? Do not buy a potted plant. Start with a Desk — built-in light, built-in irrigation, monthly water top-up. Keep it alive. Then consider a Wall.

Your space is a brand statement? A fake plant will let you down. Clients can tell. A MiniJungle Wall is a living brand statement. The difference is obvious across a room.

Gift? Gift Box. It is not a flower-shop gift box. It is collected like a designer toy — unboxed, displayed, shared. The recipient needs zero knowledge. Open. Place. Add water once a month.
