The Simple Calculation

One MiniJungle Wall costs about the same as one junior employee for one month. The productivity gain it generates is worth about one year of your recruitment budget. This is not decoration. This is infrastructure.

Non-Financial Returns

Gen Z talent: Indoor greenery is one of the top three factors for employer choice among the generation that now makes up 30%+ of the workforce (UCL, 2026).

BEAM Plus points: A living wall contributes to Indoor Environmental Quality scoring for Hong Kong's green building certification.

Social visibility: Offices with living walls see 3× the rate of organic social media sharing by employees (Artisan Moss client data). Every photo posted is free brand exposure.

ESG narrative: This is not buying carbon offsets. This is physically embedding nature into the workplace. You can put it in your sustainability report — with photos.

The Bottom Line

Office greening is not a line item in the HR budget. It is a capital investment with a payback period measured in months, not years. The question is not whether you can afford it. It is whether you can afford to have your competitors do it first.
